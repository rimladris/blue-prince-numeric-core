Blue Prince Numeric Core Calculator

Enter a 4-character code, or 4 numbers separated by spaces or commas, to find the numeric core.

Accepted inputs:
4 letters — PEAK (A=1, B=2, …)
4 numbers — 16,5,1,11 or 16 5 1 11
